Friction

Conceptual Overview

Friction is the opposing force that comes into play when one body moves or tends to move over the surface of another body. It is an electromagnetic force at the microscopic level.

Core Formulas

\[ f_s \leq \mu_s N, \quad f_k = \mu_k N \]

Key Discussion Points

  • Static friction is self-adjusting up to a limiting value.
  • Kinetic friction is generally less than limiting static friction.
  • Angle of friction and Angle of repose are key for problem-solving.
