Equations of Motion

For motion with constant acceleration, the following equations apply:

\[ v = u + at \] \[ s = ut + \frac{1}{2}at^2 \] \[ v^2 = u^2 + 2as \]

Conditions of Validity

  • Acceleration must be constant
  • Motion must be along a straight line
Equations are consequences of definitions, not independent laws.
