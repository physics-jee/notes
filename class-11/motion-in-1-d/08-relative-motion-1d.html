Relative Motion in One Dimension

Relative motion describes motion of one object as observed from another moving object.

\[ v_{A/B} = v_A - v_B \]
  • Velocities add algebraically in 1D
  • Frame choice is crucial
Relative velocity depends on observer, not on the objects alone.
