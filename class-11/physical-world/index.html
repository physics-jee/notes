Physical World

Conceptual Overview

Physics is a fundamental science that studies the basic laws of nature and their manifestation in various phenomena. It covers a vast range of scales, from subatomic particles to the entire universe.

Key Discussion Points

  • Four fundamental forces: Gravitational, Electromagnetic, Strong Nuclear, Weak Nuclear.
  • Nature of physical laws: Conservation of energy, momentum, charge, etc.
  • Scientific method: Systematic observation, controlled experiments, qualitative and quantitative reasoning.
