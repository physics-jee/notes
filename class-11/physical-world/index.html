Physical World

Understanding the scope, nature and philosophy of physics.

Chapter Overview

The Physical World chapter introduces the foundational ideas of physics. It explains what physics studies, how scientific laws are formed, and the scope and limitations of scientific knowledge.

Before solving equations, we must understand what physics is trying to describe.

Physics seeks universal laws that describe nature using minimal assumptions and mathematical precision.

How to Study This Chapter

  • Focus on conceptual clarity
  • Understand difference between law, theory and principle
  • Think philosophically — this chapter builds mindset
