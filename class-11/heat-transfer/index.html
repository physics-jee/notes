Heat Transfer

Conceptual Overview

Heat can be transferred from one place to another by three different methods: Conduction, Convection, and Radiation.

Core Formulas

\[ \frac{dQ}{dt} = -KA\frac{dT}{dx}, \quad P = \sigma e A T^4, \quad \lambda_m T = b \]

Key Discussion Points

  • Stefan-Boltzmann Law for blackbody radiation.
  • Wien's Displacement Law: Relationship between temperature and peak wavelength.
  • Newton's Law of Cooling for small temperature differences.
