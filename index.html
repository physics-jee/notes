About These Notes

These notes are designed specifically for JEE Main & Advanced aspirants. My approach follows a strict scientific hierarchy: first, we define the What (the physical concept), and then we explore the How (the mathematical application).

Strict Definitions: Every physical law is operationally defined before use.
Derivation Focused: We don't just memorize formulas; we derive them using calculus and vectors.
Modular Learning: Each chapter is a self-contained module with its own diagrams and assets.
