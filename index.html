Chapter Test: Projectile Motion

What: Definition

A projectile is an object thrown into space upon which the only acting force is gravity. The path followed by the projectile is called its trajectory.

How: The Mathematical Application

We resolve the initial velocity $u$ into two rectangular components:

  • Horizontal component: \( u_x = u \cos \theta \)
  • Vertical component: \( u_y = u \sin \theta \)

The time of flight ($T$) is derived by supporting the "What" with vertical kinematics:

\[ T = \frac{2u \sin \theta}{g} \]
