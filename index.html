About These Notes

These notes are a personal attempt to make sense of physics — especially the parts that tend to feel mechanical or mysterious when learned only through formulas.

In many places, we slow down and ask what a concept is really trying to describe, and how mathematics helps express that idea. Sometimes this means spending more time on meaning than on results.

If something feels unfamiliar or takes time to click, that’s normal. Physics often becomes clearer only after you’ve sat with an idea for a while.
