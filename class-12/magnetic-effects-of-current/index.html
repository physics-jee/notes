Magnetic Effects of Current

Conceptual Overview

Moving charges or electric currents produce magnetic fields in the surrounding space. This chapter explores the Biot-Savart Law and Lorentz Force.

Core Formulas

\[ d\vec{B} = \frac{\mu_0}{4\pi} \frac{I d\vec{l} \times \hat{r}}{r^2}, \quad \vec{F} = q(\vec{v} \times \vec{B}) \]

Key Discussion Points

  • Biot-Savart Law and Ampere's Circuital Law.
  • Magnetic field due to straight wire, circular loop, and solenoid.
  • Force between two parallel long current-carrying conductors.
  • Cyclotron frequency and motion of charge in magnetic field.
