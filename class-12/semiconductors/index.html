Semiconductor Electronics

Conceptual Overview

Semiconductors have electrical conductivity between conductors and insulators. This chapter covers the physics of p-n junctions and electronic devices.

Core Formulas

\[ n_i^2 = n_e n_h, \quad I = I_E = I_B + I_C, \quad \beta = \frac{I_C}{I_B} \]

Key Discussion Points

  • Energy bands in solids: Valence band, Conduction band, and Bandgap.
  • Extrinsic semiconductors: n-type (Doping with group V) and p-type (Doping with group III).
  • p-n Junction Diode: Forward and Reverse biasing; Rectification.
  • Logic Gates: AND, OR, NOT, NAND, NOR, and their truth tables.
