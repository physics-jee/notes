Electrostatics

Conceptual Overview

Electrostatics is the study of electric charges at rest. It covers the forces, fields, and potentials associated with stationary charges and charge distributions.

Core Formulas

\[ F = k\frac{q_1 q_2}{r^2}, \quad E = \frac{F}{q}, \quad V = k\frac{q}{r} \]

Key Discussion Points

  • Coulomb's Law: Force between two point charges in vacuum and media.
  • Electric field lines: Properties and mapping of fields.
  • Electric potential and potential energy for systems of point charges.
  • Equipotential surfaces and their relationship with electric fields.
